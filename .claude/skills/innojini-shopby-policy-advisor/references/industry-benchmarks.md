# Korean Printing Industry Benchmarks

Reference data from major Korean online printing sites for policy decision support.

## Major Players

| Company | Specialty | Key Differentiator |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| PrintCity (Megapress) | Full-service printing | Global network (US/JP/PH), 15 self-operated sites |
| AdPrint | General printing mall | AI consultation (HENEX), small-to-large volume |
| Red Printing | Goods & stickers | 500+ goods types, UV offset, 1-piece minimum |

| BizHows | SMB-focused | AI design, 650K free images, 400M+ cumulative orders |

| Adsland | Automation | 27 years, fully automated production, 10K orders/day |
| Kinko's B2B | Enterprise | 20 nationwide centers, corporate settlement |

## Membership Tier Benchmarks

### BizHows Model (Industry Standard)
| Tier | Condition (6 months) | Key Benefit |
|------|---------------------|-------------|
| FAMILY | New signup | Welcome coupon 2,000 KRW |
| VIP | 50K+ / 2+ orders | Higher accumulation rate |
| VVIP | 200K+ / 5+ orders | Additional accumulation |
| SVIP | 500K+ / 10+ orders | Monthly free shipping coupon, no downgrade |

Renewal: Monthly on 1st, based on 6-month rolling purchase history.

## Pricing Structure

### Formula
Base print cost + (Paper cost x Quantity) + Finishing cost

### Paper Cost Calculation
[Copies x (Pages / 2) / (Cuts x 500)] x Price-per-ream x (1 + Waste-rate)

### Common Surcharges
- Same-day/Express: Additional fee
- Non-standard size: Additional fee
- Special finishing: Per-item pricing

## Cancellation/Return Policy

| Stage | Cancellable? | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Before production | Yes | Within 1 hour (Snaps) |
| After production start | No | Custom product exception |
| Print defect (company fault) | Reprint/Refund | Full company responsibility |
| Customer file error | Customer pays | Reprint at customer cost |
| Simple change of mind | No | Custom products exempt |

Legal: E-commerce Act Art.17 - Custom products exempt from 7-day return with prior consent.
